Privacy Policy for the WoodMaster App

Last Updated: May 19, 2025

Thank you for using WoodMaster (hereinafter referred to as the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and protect it. By using our App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

The WoodMaster App is designed for calculating the volumes of round timber and firewood according to the tables provided in ДСТУ-4020-2-2001 "Лісоматеріали круглі та пиляні. Методи обмірювання та визначення об'ємів. Частина 2. Лісоматеріали круглі" та ТУ У 16.1-00994207-005:2018 "Деревина дров’яна. Технічні умови"

1. Information We Collect and In-App Purchases

The WoodMaster App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or non-personal information about you or your use of the App.

All data you enter into the App for timber volume calculations are processed and stored exclusively locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

We do not use cookies, tracking tools, or analytics services.

The WoodMaster App does not contain any in-app purchases. All functionality of the App is available immediately after installation without the need for additional payments.

2. Use and Sharing of Information

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use it or share it with third parties. Your experience using the App is entirely private and confined to your device.

3. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. We advise you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
